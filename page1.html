Soup

Published: 31 May 2025

History & Origin
Soup is one of humanity’s oldest dishes, traced to hot-stone cooking in the Neolithic era roughly 20 000 years ago. As cultures blossomed, so did regional soups: miso in Japan, borscht in Eastern Europe, pho in Vietnam…

Cultural Significance
Across the globe, soup often signals comfort and hospitality. A steaming bowl says “welcome home.”
